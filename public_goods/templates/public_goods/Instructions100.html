Instructions

In this study, you will be in a randomly formed group of 3 participants, some of which may be robots. Each participant in the group is given 100 points, but you are the group leader, which was decided based on your performance in the previous task. You will go first. A point is equal to .01 cents.
The group has the opportunity to undertake a joint project. Each participant in the group decides how much they are going to contribute to the project. Contribution could be any integer from 0 to their total amount of points.
The earnings from the project are calculated as follows: The contributions of all of the participants are added up, the total contribution is multiplied by two, and the resulting amount is the total earnings from the project. These total earnings are evenly split among all of the participants. Your payoff equals your earnings from the project, plus the amount you did not contribute. Remember that how many points you end up with will affect your bonus payment.
